Seattle, located in the state of Washington, is the largest city in the Pacific Northwest. It's known for its stunning natural surroundings, including Puget Sound, the Olympic Mountains, and the Cascade Range. The city was founded in 1851 and named after Chief Seattle of the local Duwamish and Suquamish tribes
Seattle is a major port and a gateway to Asia and Alaska. It's also a hub for technology and internet-based commerce, with companies like Microsoft and Amazon headquartered there. The city is famous for landmarks like the Space Needle, built for the 1962 World's Fair, and its vibrant cultural scene
